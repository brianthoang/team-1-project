The Pokémon Trading Card Game: Online Illustration Exhibition is now open through October 23, 2022 for all Pokémon fans. This virtual trading card game allows fans to interact with the unique world of Pokémon without stepping a foot outside the fan's home. There are three different themes for the fans to engage with: Life, History, and Artist. Through these themes, fans can learn about the habitats and routines of Pokémon, the evolution of Pokémon, and the steps that the artists use in order to capture their work.

About

Hello! This is a site that is created to give users access to a Pokémon Database by using PokeApi dataset. Through this database, you are able to browse through several different Pokémon characters and games. The database is separated into each generation and the games are separated into the different available Pokémon games. In addition, further information can be shown such as various statistics, Pokémon types, and so much more! The Pokémon community has so many fanbases on several platforms, so our site allows you to view other Pokémon communities in order to be up to date with the latest news!

Pokémon (which is an abbreviation for "Pocket Monsters") is a Japanese franchise ran by Nintendo that orginally started out as a game where game players would take the role of Pokémon trainers and collect different Pokémon characters and use them to battle other Pokémons. Pokémon was created by a man named Satoshi Tajiri and is illustator friend, Ken Sugimori. When Satoshi Tajiri started to make his own video games, he was influenced by his childhood passion of exploring the forests and finding different bugs and tadpoles, which ultimately made him pitch this idea to Nintendo. Fast forward to today, Pokémon is one of the most successful and well-known video game franchises in the world.
